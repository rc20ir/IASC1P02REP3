Jump to Panda Characteristics

Pandas are very unique creatures and are descendants of the bear family. They are fluffy white and black creatures that carry various skills such as: excellent camouflage for their habitat such as snow, they are great climbers and swimmers, and they have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food.

Despite their lack of natural predators, pandas are still at risk. There are only 1800 pandas in the wild currently known and it's mainly due to habitat loss. Wild pandas once roamed bamboo forests in China, Vietnam, and Laos. Today, found only in China.

Top 3 Panda Foods

  1. Bamboo (around 12-38kg per day)
  2. Wild fruits (huge apple lovers)
  3. Vegetables (fans of carrots)

Panda Characteristics

  • Black and white coat to keep them warm
  • Adult pandas are usually 150cm from nose to rump
  • Most of their time is spent alone
